Copilot Scoring Formula

Each property is scored using a weighted formula across price, size, bathrooms, and match quality.

Score = (0.4 × matchQuality) + (0.3 × size/100) + (0.2 × bathrooms/3) + (0.1 × 1/(price/100000)) Columns exported: → Price → Size (m²) → Bathrooms → Zone → Match Quality → Computed Score
